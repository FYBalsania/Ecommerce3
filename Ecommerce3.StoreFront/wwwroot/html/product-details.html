About Brown Linseeds

Brown linseeds are rich in fibre and essential fatty acids, the seeds can be ground down to make flaxseed which can be used in baking as an egg replacement for vegans. Linseeds are a product of the flax plant (Linum usitatissimum) and contain numerous beneficial components that can help your daily diets.

Benefits of Brown Linseeds:

  • Raw product, so can be used in several ways
  • Easy to add to your daily diet
  • Suitable for vegans and vegetarians

Usage Instructions

Best used for baking and adding to bread recipes. Should be heated prior to consumption.

Allergens

May contain traces of milk, nuts (including peanuts), seeds (including sesame seeds & mustard seeds), cereals, celery, soya & gluten.

What are the main health benefits of Linseeds?

SpiceMart Team Answered

Linseeds are rich in omega-3 fatty acids, fibre, and plant-based protein. They help lower cholesterol, improve digestion, regulate blood sugar, and support heart health. Regular use may also promote healthy skin and hair.

How can I include Linseeds in my daily diet?

SpiceMart Team Answered

You can sprinkle ground linseeds on salads, yogurt, or cereal, mix them into smoothies, or use them in baking. Always prefer ground linseeds over whole seeds for better nutrient absorption.
